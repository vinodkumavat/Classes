ABOUT KUMAVAT

Kumavat is one of the Best Classes in Vasai and Virar for JEE (MAIN + ADVANCE), PRE MEDICAL (NEET/AIIMS), Competitive Exams and Olympiads.

The faculty is a combination of strong academic expertise and immense knowledge. Here at KUMAVAT, we conduct periodic tests that are similar to the pattern of JEE/NEET examination which make our students comfortable and confident enough to sit for the JEE (Main & Advanced) and NEET exam without any pressure.

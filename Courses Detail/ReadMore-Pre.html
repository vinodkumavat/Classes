PRE-NURTURE & CAREER FOUNDATION

This course is for the students of classes 6th to 10th who wish to prepare for various Competitive Exams and Olympiads. It nourishes Creativity, Thinking Ability, Competitve Nature, and Divergent Tendency in the students through our Classroom Contact Programs and Workshops.

This course assures the success/improvement of students in their school exams, boards, NTSE and several national/international Olympiads such as NSEP/NSEB/NSEC/NSEA, IJSO, NMTC, RMO and private Olympiads like IMO, NSO, NSTSE, etc.

Pre-Nurture and Career Foundation course have been specifically designed to prepare students of class 6th to 10th by forming a solid base of every concept. Here at KUMAVAT, we conduct periodic tests that are planned in such a way which appraises the depth of students' learning and reports of the test give a detail information on areas or subjects which need to be sculptured and polished.

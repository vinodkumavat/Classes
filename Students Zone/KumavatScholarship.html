KUMAVAT SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAMME

To motivate, reward and financially assist the deserving and meritorious students, Kumavat offers two kinds of the Scholarships under KUMAVAT SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAMME (KSP). All scholarships are applicable for the Academic Session 2018-Till Date. These are:



1. Special Scholarship Programme (SSP)

The objective behind SSP is to reward, nurture and financially support the wtudents who are having outstanding talent & intelligence required to succeed in JEE (Advanced). The financial support will be proficiently complimented by systematic Classroom coaching for JEE (Advanced) at Kumavat. The SSP shall equip the student to focus purely on JEE (Advanced) preparation without facing any financial burden (Zero Cost) by reimbursing his/her Pre-JEE (Advanced).

2. General Scholarship Programme (GSP)

This Scholarship Programme are applicable to:

  • To the Tuition Fee part of Total Course Free.
  • To the students of All the KUMAVAT Study Centres.
  • To the students of the Year Classroom Contact Program (YCCPs).
  • To the students of JEE (Main + Advanced), JEE (MAIN) & Pre-Medical division.

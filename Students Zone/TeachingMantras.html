  Revision Lecture:

4. Board Lectures "SAMANVAYA"

  • Flexible programme covering all subject with board expert.
  • Guideline for the monthly pattern to encourage their performance in board exam of JEE concept with SAMANVAYA.


1. PMP (Practice Makes Perfect)

  1. Every lecture followed by PMP sheet having current topic & previous taught topic for revision and practice.
  2. These PMPs have mono & multi conceptual problem to enhance speed, in-depth understanding, application of concept and self-revision.

2. BOOKLETS (PUSTIKA)

  1. Topic wise booklets designed according to JEE syllabus mains & advanced.
  2. Contains theory in nutshell to revise.
  3. Contains Tricky, Brain Storming and previous year IIT JEE objective & subjective question.

3. QUESTION BANKS.

  1. "SAFAL" (Systematic Approach For Advanced Learning)
  2. "FILE" Contents for extreme tricky multi conceptual question followed by IIT Advanced/AIIMS question.
  3. Separate booklets for MHTCET.


5. Examination "PARAKH"

Regular Examination will be conducted by Kumavat for students to complete nationally and to improve their potential & performance. The questions asked in tests will be of high level and tougher than those asked in competitive exams which will give them an extra edge over other students.

To be familiar with JEE exam pattern, tests are conducted periodically.

  • UNIT TEST
  • JEE MAINS TEST
  • JEE ADVANCE (MAJOR & MINOR)
  • BOARD PATTERN TEST
  • KNTS (Kumavat National Test Series)

*Series test along with discussion classes for final touch to boost their rank.
